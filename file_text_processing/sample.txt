Several cricket experts from the Star Sports expert panel for the IPL 2026 went on to pick the likely possible options for the Purple Cap. 
They did not differ much in their choices, at least for the majority.

Former RCB captain Faf du Plessis picked Varun Chakravarthy, the Kolkata Knight Riders spinner, as the ideal bowler to end up with a purple cap at the end of the season. 
Harbhajan Singh and Piyush Chawla, the former KKR spinners, also backed the veteran spinner.

Varun Chakravarthy did not have the best season in the ICC T20 World Cup 2026, but still, high hopes are pinned on him.
Cheteshwar Pujara, Saba Karim, and former CSK pacer Lakshmipati Balaji also backed the KKR star.
